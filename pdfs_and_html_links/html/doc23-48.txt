Your gift makes it sing . Whether you're a longtime opera lover or just love being part of something special, this is your chance to say, “I helped make that happen.” With a Piece by Piece sponsorship, you’re not just supporting a production—you’re stepping into the spotlight. Your gift lights Mimi’s candle, stirs the snow in Act III, and wraps the lovers in music and moonlight. Sponsorships start at just $65! How it works: Pick your piece —props, costumes, scenes, arias, and more! Your name will shine on our website and in pre-show donor emails! Pull back the curtain and receive behind-the-scenes photos, intimate designer insights, exclusive invites, and glimpses of the magic as it’s being created. SPONSOR A PIECE Mimi’s Candle — $65 A single flame, trembling in the dark, becomes the start of everything. Nearly ten candles are used throughout the run of this production—each one keeping Mimi’s world glowing with warmth and hope. Your gift helps bring this symbol to life.

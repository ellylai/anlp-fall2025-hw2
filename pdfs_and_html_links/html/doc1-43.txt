PPG Paints Arena Main Team Store 1001 Fifth Avenue Pittsburgh, PA 15219 412.553.8553 The PensGear main team store in PPG Paints Arena is open Monday through Friday (10 AM - 3 PM) and during all Penguins home games. On weekday game days, the store will stay open until the lobby is cleared before doors open. The store is only open during game days on the weekends. Open store times will vary based on game time. The 4,000 square foot store is stocked with the latest Penguins merchandise featuring brands such as Adidas, New Era, 47 Brand, and more! Many items are designs exclusively for PensGear, making our stores a one-of-a-kind shopping experience. Other locations are open throughout the arena during games. The main team store is located inside the Highmark Gate entrance, which is along Centre Avenue near Epiphany Church and the intersection with Washington Place.

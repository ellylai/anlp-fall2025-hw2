Whether you’re new to this area or a frequent guest, we can’t wait to share the magic of live events with you. The Cultural District is a project of the Pittsburgh Cultural Trust’s history. Dotted with theaters, restaurants, galleries, and public art, this neighborhood is home to Downtown’s dynamic arts, entertainment, and dining scene. Here, you’ll discover a spectrum of choices for entertainment, arts, and enlightenment including contemporary music, modern dance, visual art, and thought-provoking theater, as well as classical music, opera, ballet, popular musical theater, art film and more. You can find new and exciting things to do year-round in the Cultural District.
